# Quickstart

Get your workspace running in minutes.

1. **Sign in** to your account.  
2. **Connect** your retail marketplaces (Shopee, Lazada, TikTok Shop).  
3. **Create your first campaign** using the Automation tool.  
4. **Set up alerts** for budget thresholds and performance triggers.  
5. **Invite your team** and share dashboards.
