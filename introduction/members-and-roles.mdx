Epsilo workspaces are built for collaboration.\
Each member has a defined role that controls what they can view, edit, or automate.

Use this guide to understand available roles, assign permissions, and keep your workspace secure.

## Overview

Every workspace includes four main roles:

| Role       | Access level                                                            | Best for                        |
| ---------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| **Owner**  | Full access to billing, workspace settings, and storefront connections. | Business or team lead           |
| **Admin**  | Manage members, create and edit automations, and view all campaigns.    | Performance or operations lead  |
| **Member** | Run campaigns, view dashboards, and trigger automations.                | Daily operator                  |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to dashboards and reports.                             | External partners or management |

## Security Recommendations

- **Use company email domains** for all invites.
- Enable **two-factor authentication** for Owners and Admins.
- Review member lists quarterly and remove inactive users.
- Limit Viewer access to external agencies or auditors.
